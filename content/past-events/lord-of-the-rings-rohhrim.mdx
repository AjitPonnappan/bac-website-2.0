<center>**FORTH EORLINGAS!**</center>

Bengaluru Anime Club (BAC) hosted an exclusive fan screening of *The Lord of the Rings: The War of the Rohirrim*. Set prior to the events of the main books, this is a fresh take on the story of Helm Hammerhand of Rohan from legendary anime director Kenji Kamiyama (*Ghost in the Shell: Stand-Alone Complex*, *Moribito*, *Eden of the East*).

The screening took place on December 15th at INOX Garuda Mall, in collaboration with <a href="https://store.anisync.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">AniSync</a> and <a href="https://www.pvrcinemas.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">PVR INOX</a>. <a href="https://www.wbd.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Warner Bros.</a> offered posters to the attendees, who numbered 55+. Local Nagatoro-san and BAC admin Mahi interviewed the attendees, and Futtaim conducted a *Lord of the Rings* quiz, with two teams of pairs taking home two copies of the official *War of the Rohirrim* art-book and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gashapon" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Gacha balls</a> respectively, sponsored by AniSync.
